## What AurPay Actually Does

AurPay is a global blockchain payment platform designed for businesses that want to accept cryptocurrency — Bitcoin, Ethereum, USDT, USDC, DAI, TRX, and 50+ other assets — without the usual headaches.

The way it works is pretty simple. A customer clicks "pay with crypto" on your site. AurPay shows them a QR code and the exact amount in their chosen crypto. They scan, they pay. You get the funds directly to your wallet. No intermediary sitting on your money. Settlement happens fast — not T+3 like a bank wire.

What makes it different from something like Coinbase Commerce? One word: gas fees. On Coinbase's model, if you process 1,000 ERC-20 transactions, you pay gas 1,000 times. At ~$2 per transaction, that's $2,000 in gas alone. AurPay's smart contract batches those 1,000 transactions and charges gas once. One payment. ~$2. The math is not subtle.
